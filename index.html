Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in CSE Discipline, IIT Palakkad. I did my PhD from Department of CSA, IISc under the guidance of Prof. Y.N Srikant. Prior to joining to IIT Palakkad I worked as research fellow at SUTD, Singapore.
